  text=Blockquotes — Nested
  text=Blockquotes use > prefixes. They can be nested by stacking multiple > characters.

  text=The best way to get a project done faster is to start sooner.

  text=Multi-Paragraph Blockquote
  text=Coroutines are a way of doing cooperative multitasking. Unlike threads, they don't block the underlying OS thread; they suspend execution and yield control back to the caller.
  text=This means you can have thousands of coroutines running on just a handful of threads, making them much more memory-efficient than traditional thread-per-request models.
  text=Nested Blockquote
  text=Outer quote: Here is the original statement that was made in a previous message.
  text=Inner quote: And this is a reply or elaboration nested inside the first quote.
  text=The inner quote can also span multiple paragraphs.
  text=We return to the outer quote level here, after the nested section.
  text=Three Levels Deep
  text=Level 1 — original message
  text=Level 2 — first reply
  text=Level 3 — reply to the reply
  text=This is the deepest level in this example.
  text=Back to level 2.
  text=Back to level 1.
