  text=Autolinks
  text=Raw URLs written between angle brackets are autolinks: https://example.com. They produce a clickable link whose text is the URL itself. links=https://example.com
  text=Email autolinks also work: <user@example.com>. The renderer should produce a mailto: link.
  text=Link Destinations
  text=Links can point to relative paths: README. They can also contain query strings: search. links=../README.md|https://example.com/search?q=kotlin&lang=en
  text=A link destination with a fragment: section anchor. These are especially common in long document navigation. links=#heading-level-2
